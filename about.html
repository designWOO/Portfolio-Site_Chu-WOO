My story

Nice to meet you!

I started as a graphic designer for printing everything related to promotional material: like business cards, brochures, the user manuals which included many pages. But there was a limitation in printed media to express my idea in various ways, and that is why I started studying Web Design and Web Development. Finally, I've mastered HTML5 and CSS by self-taught and started working at a web agency. I was able to do it myself because I have a background in Computer Engineering from university and already realized how important UX/UI design is through my design experience. For this reason, I could understand the web field easily. My recent particular interest is the animate function in HTML so I keep going practicing JavaScript and SVG images. I will make an enjoyable website using my ability to design and coding.
